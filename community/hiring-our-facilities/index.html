Hiring our facilities

Host your event at The Knox School

Home | Community | Hiring our facilities

We are proud to offer groups

from across our community access to our state-of-the-art campus facilities, including:

A sports centre with two full size basketball courts and a weights room, adjacent to an oval with cricket pitch.

A 389-seat auditorium with spacious foyer and AV facilities.

The Robert Read Learning Centre and multi-purpose area. Located on the top floor of our administration building this is well-lit, carpeted and heated.

The school also has a variety of other facilities available for hire, such as classrooms and other common areas.
